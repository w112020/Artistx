Who Am I?

I’m a self taught artist with no learning experience, I started making artwork because of some art timelapse videos. I started with putting a piece of paper on the laptop to trace images/other art and calling it my own, but ever since my mother convinced me to start making drawings on my own, I’ve been slowly practicing on drawing things on my own without tracing. I’ve started with Dragon Ball and when creating an artstyle, it looks pretty similar to that style (Mainly similar to the DBS Broly artstyle made by Naohiro Shintani).

What I Offer

I also offer small art tutoring servises for those who want to learn the basics of art drawing. Services may include learning the basic steps of drawing a body, hair, proper sizes for each body part, etc.
